How the Game Works:

  • Select level you'd like to play at.
  • When you press Start Game, the countdown clock starts (top right corner). You have one minute to accurately type as many words as you can.
  • When a letter is green, you typed that letter correctly.
  • When a letter is red, you typed the wrong letter. Press the Backspace key and try again. You won't be allowed to move on to the next sentence until you correct the mistake.
