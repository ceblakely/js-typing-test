How You're Scored

Gross WPM = ((All Correctly Typed Keys) / 5) / Time in Minutes

Gross WPM calculates how fast you can type without counting in errors. I chose to utilize Gross WPM because you cannot go to the next round if you have any errors in your sentence.

Accuracy = ((Length of sentence - errors) / Length of Sentence) * 100

This metric determines how accurate all of your keystrokes are.
